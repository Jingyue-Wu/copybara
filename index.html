Clipboard

About

Enables you to copy text to clipboard from anything. This includes images, videos, and copy-blocked pages and documents.

Also allows you to access past copy history and manually save text to a virtual clipboard.

Data is synchronized across all browsers that you are logged in to, letting you quickly save/transfer links, bookmarks and other info across devices.

Instructions

Copying

Click the "Select from webpage" button or use Ctrl + Shift + S to activate the scanner. Drag your cursor over the area you want to copy. The text will automatically be copied.

If the result has errors, zoom in and try again.


Virtual Clipboard

Use the "Save to clipboard" field to manually save text.

Use the "Clear" button to delete your saved data from all browser instances.
